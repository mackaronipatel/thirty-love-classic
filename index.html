The Event

Welcome to Camp

Tyler and Nikhil are turning 30, and we're celebrating with a long weekend at an incredible house in Suncadia, right in the Cascade Mountains outside Seattle.

The vibe is tennis camp meets summer hangout — we'll have a tennis coach for lessons, plenty of time to rally and play, plus all the outdoor games you could want: spikeball, volleyball, you name it. No pressure, no intensity — just good times and good people.

When you're not on the court, there's a private sauna, steam room, cold plunge, hot tub, and a whole lot of mountain views to enjoy. Come hang.

The House

Suncadia Spa Lodge

We booked one of the top-rated homes in Suncadia — a modern mountain lodge with 4,000 sq ft of living space, 30-foot ceilings, and golf course views. This place has everything.

The house features a private dry sauna, steam room, cold plunge, and hot tub. There's a game room with ping-pong and foosball, a chef's kitchen with a massive island, and over 1,000 sq ft of covered outdoor living space with a gas fire pit and BBQ.
